Prospect → Package Match

How to read a business, match the pain, and pitch the right JALG package
🎯

Lead Capture

$1,500 + $87/mo
Good foundation, no automation. They answer sometimes but have no speed-to-lead or missed call text-back. Just need the basics.
⚡

Lead Machine

$2,800 + $147/mo
Gaps in capture AND nurture. No follow-up sequences, weak review generation, mediocre web presence. Default pick when in doubt.
🔥

Full Growth

$4,200 + $197/mo
Hurting across the board. Bad site, weak reviews, no booking, no automation, past customers untouched. Max pain = max opportunity.

When Signals Conflict → Go Higher

If a business has 200 reviews but a broken website, they're Full Growth — not Lead Capture. The weakest link determines the package. When in doubt between two tiers, always recommend the higher one.
Secret Shopper Checklist — 5 Min Before Every Loom
01

Google Search

2 MIN
Search "[service] [area]" — note their map pack position
Search their business name — is GBP info correct?
Count reviews vs top 3 competitors — write down numbers
02

GBP Deep Check

1 MIN
Photo count — under 10 = weak
Last GBP post — 3+ months ago = stale
Read recent 1-star reviews — owner responding?
Services, description, service area filled?
03

Website Walk

1 MIN
Open on mobile — does it load fast and look right?
Can you book or request a quote in 10 seconds?
Any reviews/testimonials displayed on site?
Chat widget or text option?
04

The Call Test

2 MIN
Call with *67 — answered? How many rings?
If voicemail — start timer, wait for callback/text
Try submitting contact form — any confirmation?
Note response time — this is your #1 Loom talking point
Loom Recording Rules
🎬

3 Tabs Open

Google search results, their GBP listing, their website. Screen share + face cam on. Nothing else visible.

⏱

Under 4 Min

Lead with biggest pain in first 30 seconds. Walk through evidence. Close with clear next step. Don't ramble.

🚫

No Package Names

Never say "Lead Machine" or prices. Describe the solution. Pricing comes up on the sales call, not in the Loom.

📤

Send Immediately

FB DM the Loom link the moment you finish recording. Don't batch. Speed wins. One video, one send, next prospect.

Talk Tracks — What to Say in Each Loom
Lead Capture Pitch

"Great work — you just need to catch the leads you're missing"

Open: "Your reviews are solid and your reputation speaks for itself. But when I tried to reach you..."
Pain: "I called, got voicemail, nothing happened. If your competitor texts back in 60 seconds, I'm going with them."
Fix: "I'd build a system where missed calls and form submissions get instant text-backs automatically."
Close: "Would you be open to 15 minutes to see if it makes sense for your business?"
Lead Machine Pitch

"You're losing leads at two points — first touch and follow-up"

Open: "I searched [service] near [area] and found you. You've got [X] reviews — but your competitor has [Y]."
Pain 1: "Contact form but no real booking. Customers submit and wait. Some call your competitor instead."
Pain 2: "Last review was [X] ago. Without a system asking every customer, that gap grows every month."
Close: "I build this specifically for home service businesses in NoVA. Free to chat this week?"
Full Growth Pitch

"Your work is great but your online presence doesn't match"

Open: "I searched [service] in your area and it took me a while to find you. Your competitors show up first."
Pain 1: "Your website [is outdated / broken on mobile / has no booking]. Customers can't book in 30 seconds, they leave."
Pain 2: "You have [X] reviews. Top 3 competitors have [Y, Z, W]. Google ranks them higher."
Close: "I'd love to show you what a full system would look like. 15 minutes this week?"
